Take a Chance on Photography

Chance Photography was founded in Toronto in 2002 as a family owned business. Since then, we have opened 3 other locations across Ontario and have served over 200 clients. We offer a full range of photo services and personalized merchandise that leave every client satisfied with their end product.

We provide the ultimate experience – an original photo session, customized to your liking. Our photographers guide you while you turn your best moments into the best memories. Whether you are posing alone or with your loved ones, your experience will be as memorable as the photos themselves. We create photos you will cherish for years to come.

Our photographers’ talent and our creative direction result in poses that burst with life. From portraits of babies, couples or pets, to high quality photos of your home, Chance Photography does it all. Our photographers are devoted to capturing life’s most valuable moments and eternalizing them in an artistic manner. We create photographic stories that are tailored to each client.

We offer:

  • fully customisable, simply decorated, studio spaces in neutral tones that can be used as is or customized by you
  • several pieces of furniture and home decorations ranging from vintage to modern that can easily be moved around to dress your set or bring in your own
  • a growing, broad and ever-changing collection of props, and furniture
